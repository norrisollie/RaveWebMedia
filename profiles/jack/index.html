Platinum PT

Platinum PT is aimed for intermediate to advanced fitness individuals or those with more disposable income. The aim is to offer a premium level of personal training to clients.

My main task for this project was to create a dynamic, custom WordPress theme which allows certain content sections to be editable by my client.

Throughout this project I also branded the business choosing the colour scheme and logo, wireframing the initial website designs and user testing as I went along.

Nexa is a piece of Digital Marketing I created for a Squarespace theme.

MyMuseum

MyMuseum is a social platform designed to create a unique experience tailored for you and your trips to various museums (such as the National Maritime Museum in Greenwich, London). With the ability to like artifacts, interact with other members of the museum, Share experiences and stories, collect notes and take pictures.
