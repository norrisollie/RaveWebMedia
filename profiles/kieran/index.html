Bio

Joseph Dunkerley is a professional voxel artist specialising in experimental animation and game design. Over the years he has worked on numerous experimental games, intricate animations and surrealist art based projects. His work includes ‘Critical Annihilation’, ‘Orchardstra’, ‘Swamp Alchemy’, the ‘Mega Isometric Block Pack’ and more.
